Mocha Martini

Mocha martini is made with great espresso and is a superb, richly flavoured balance of sweet and bitter.

Ingredients:

  • 45 mL Bourbon whiskey
  • 30 mL Espresso coffee
  • 15 mL Irish cream liqueur
  • 15 mL Dark creme de cacao liqueur
  • 15 mL Single cream
  • Garnish: chocolate powder

Steps:

  1. Shake whisky, espresso coffee, irish cream liqueur and dark creme de cacao liqueur with ice and fine strain into chilled glass
  2. Float cream in centre of drink
  3. Dust with chocolate powder
  4. Enjoy!
