Eastside

Eastside is a fresh gin cocktail that is a subtle variation on the Southside. The addition of muddled cucumber to the citrus and mint makes this cocktail a delight.

Ingredients:

  • 60 mL gin
  • 30 mL freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 22.5 mL simple syrup
  • 6 - 8 Mint Leaves
  • Cucumber

Steps:

  1. Gently muddle the mint and cucumber in your cocktail shaker
  2. Add the remaining ingredients
  3. Shake with ice
  4. Double strain over fresh ice into a double old fashioned
  5. Garnish with a cucumber ribbon and mint sprig
  6. Enjoy!
